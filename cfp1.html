Paper Submission:

Prospective authors are invited to submit original research papers in electronic format through conference website. All submissions must be in English only. Papers for both oral presentations should comply with the IEEE format and specifications. Authors are requested to submit a full paper using the IEEE conference template. The IEEE paper template can be downloaded from https://www.ieee.org/conferences/publishing/templates.html.

Best Paper Award :

Best papers award(s) will be presented to authors whose work represents groundbreaking research in their respective areas.
All accepted papers MUST be presented (based on the chosen mode of presentation) by one of the authors, or, if none of the authors are able to attend, by a qualified surrogate.
