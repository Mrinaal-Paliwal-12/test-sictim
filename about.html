9th SOMAIYA INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON TECHNOLOGY AND INFORMATION MANAGEMENT (SICTIM) 2023

Current Theme :

Value Creation through Digital Transformation

Planned Dates: 19th and 20th January 2023
Event Type: Conference
Audience Type: Academicians, Corporate Managers, Faculty, Researchers, Students

Digital disruption is changing most of the business functions and processes to sustain in the current competitive global markets in order to lead the respective domains. Today’s digital era is demanding individual business leaders and organizations to adapt themselves to emerging trends in technologies for innovative business strategies and to reach out to untouched potential stakeholders digitally as well as physically. Business leaders are towards creating maximum value out of every business function through digital transformation in various possible ways.

9th Edition of SICTIM will be platform where Academicians, corporate managers, research scholars and students can attend, network and present their innovative research in the various tracks like Data Science Technologies, Business Analytics, Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning etc. We are in the process of collaboration with IEEE Mumbai chapter and IEEE Xplore Digital library and few foreign universities and delegates.

Our brochure

  • Conference Objectives are to offer a platform for marketing practitioners, academicians and researchers to
  • Discuss contemporary issues and opportunities in the area of customer journeys and marketing.
  • Discuss various strategic options to overcome the challenges in mapping customer journeys, competition, and changing consumer behaviour.
  • Explore scholarly research on changes in customer journeys, collaborations and marketing across the globe.
